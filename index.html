Making instalment payments

Some individuals and most businesses must pay their current-year taxes in periodic instalments.

For general information about instalment payments go to: Instalment payments for individuals (opens in a new window)Instalment payments for corporations (opens in a new window)Instalment payments for GST/HST (opens in a new window)

A COVID-19 benefit or subsidy payment arrangement requires approval

You will need to contact the collections line for individuals at 1-833-253-7615collections business line at 1-833-615-2087 if you need to make a payment arrangement to repay this COVID-19 benefit or subsidy.

Contact the CRA to set up a payment arrangement

If you wish to make multiple payments to pay this amount, you will need to contact the CRA to advise us that you want to set up a payment arrangement.
